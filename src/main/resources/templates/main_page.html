What is Satbify?

Satbify redefines the concept of a "chord track". It maks you compose better straight away, offering an intuitive way to manipulate chords, scales, and tonalities by simply drawing keySwitches in the MIDI editor. Once these keySwitches are in place, Satbify creates four separate parts following the rules of traditional voice-leading.

Each voice is placed on a separate track, making it effortless to route to individual virtual instruments or incorporate into your arrangement as distinct voices. This functionality enables creative experimentation with fresh harmonies, modulations, and voicings, all in an automated and efficient manner.

Satbify is designed to be both fast and flexible. For those well-versed in music theory, it automates the routine aspects of composition. For beginners, it provides an invaluable learning tool, encouraging experimentation, listening, and rapid skill development. By bringing professional composition techniques to your fingertips, Satbify opens up new expressive possibilities for every musician.
